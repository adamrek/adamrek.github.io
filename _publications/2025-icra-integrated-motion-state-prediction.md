**Authors:** **Kai Ren**, Yuichi Nakamura, Kazuaki Kondo, Kei Shimonishi, Takahide Ito, Jun-ichiro Furukawa, Qi An

This paper proposes an integrated motion state prediction framework for sit-to-stand and stand-to-sit movements, aiming to improve the effectiveness of power assist control. The proposed method contributes to more accurate and responsive human–robot interaction in assistive systems.
